# Banner image checklist

Odoo Apps Store listing needs `banner.png` here. Specs:

- **Filename**: `banner.png`
- **Size**: 1280 × 480 px (standard) or 560 × 315 px (small variant)
- **Format**: PNG, sRGB
- **Weight**: < 200 KB recommended
- **Content**: ATGO logo + "Cloud attendance for ZKTeco — in Odoo"
- **Background**: solid dark or light, NOT transparent

Also recommended:

- `icon.png` (140×140 px) — sometimes shown alongside the listing
- `screenshots/*.png` — optional, picked up automatically by Odoo Apps

Avoid:

- Trademarked Odoo logos / colors as decorative elements
- Photos of real people / employees without consent
- Brands of ZKTeco models you don't have permission to picture

Until you have a real banner, the listing renders without one — Odoo just
shows the module name + summary.
